Why this roadmap

PSAT 10 in spring is a free diagnostic — it doesn't count toward anything official. The PSAT/NMSQT next fall is the one that actually qualifies for National Merit recognition. With only about six to seven months between them, this site is built to learn each concept once, well, then drill it before the test that counts.
